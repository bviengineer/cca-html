Call a Friend

I stopped running and thought to myself. Kaisa can quickly tell me what she hears and how to fight the suspected attacker or stalker. Wait a minute...why do I hear a cell phone rininging loudly behind of me? Let me hang up and try again. I dialed, 284-495-2289. I gasped! Kaisa? Is that you? As my walking came to a sudden and abrupt hault, slowly out of the Shadows, emerged Kaisa, she was trying to catch up with me so we can walk home together, but she had lost her voice and wasn't able to shout.

Whew...I thought to myself, what if?. I was very happy to see her.
