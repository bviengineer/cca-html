British Virgin Islands

The British Virgin Islands is a made up of an archipelago of about 60 Islands, cays and rocks. The B.V.I. are territories of the United Kingdom.

There are four main Islands:

  • Tortola
  • Virgin Gorda
  • Jost Van Dyke
  • Anegada
